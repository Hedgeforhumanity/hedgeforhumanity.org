What is the H4H Fund?
A global wealth fund awarding one-year-long, $100/month basic incomes to random participants from around the world. The H4H fund assets are held in a multi signature gnosis safe and earn interest using select yield generating protocols such as Yearn.finance. The objective is to retain principle and use the interest generated to fund the basic incomes.
Global Citizen
We are all equal citizens of the Earth—regardless of our different nationalities, languages, ethnicities, and traditions.
Basic Income
A basic income is a periodic cash payment delivered individually to every human—no strings attached.
A Crucial Project
It is time to show the impact of a global basic income—and to test out the technology that can make it a reality.
